SECTION 1 — SERVICES

1.1 Service Description

NexaTel agrees to provide the following telecommunications products and services to Meridian Financial Group:

  a) NexaTel Unified Connect Pro — Enterprise VoIP platform supporting up to 1,200 concurrent users with 99.99% uptime SLA.
  b) NexaTel SecureLink SD-WAN — Software-defined wide area network solution connecting 18 branch office locations across the United States.
  c) NexaTel CloudPBX — Cloud-hosted private branch exchange with advanced call routing, IVR, and analytics dashboard.
  d) NexaTel DataShield DDoS Protection — Always-on volumetric and application-layer attack mitigation service.
  e) Dedicated Internet Access (DIA) — 10 Gbps symmetrical fiber connection at Meridian's primary data center in Chicago, Illinois.

1.2 Service Locations

Services will be delivered at the following locations:
  - Primary Data Center: 500 Commerce Boulevard, Chicago, IL 60601
  - Secondary Data Center: 8800 West Corporate Drive, Naperville, IL 60563
  - Regional Offices: Dallas TX, Atlanta GA, New York NY, San Francisco CA (18 branches total)

SECTION 3 — SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENT

3.1 Uptime Guarantee

NexaTel commits to the following service availability levels:

  - NexaTel Unified Connect Pro: 99.99% monthly uptime (≤ 4.38 minutes downtime/month)
  - NexaTel SecureLink SD-WAN: 99.95% monthly uptime per site
  - Dedicated Internet Access: 99.99% monthly uptime

3.2 Mean Time to Repair (MTTR)

  - Priority 1 (Complete outage): 4-hour MTTR
  - Priority 2 (Degraded service): 8-hour MTTR
  - Priority 3 (Minor issue): 48-hour MTTR

3.3 Service Credits

In the event NexaTel fails to meet the uptime guarantees stated in Section 3.1, Meridian shall be entitled to service credits calculated as follows:
  - 99.9% – 99.99%: 5% credit of monthly fee for affected service
  - 99.0% – 99.9%: 10% credit
  - Below 99.0%: 25% credit

4.1 Communications Act Compliance

SECTION 5 — TECHNOLOGY SPECIFICATIONS

5.1 Network Infrastructure

The SD-WAN solution shall operate using NexaTel's Tier 1 backbone network leveraging BGP routing protocol with MPLS traffic engineering. The solution uses OpenFlow-enabled switching infrastructure compatible with NexaTel's Software-Defined Networking Controller Platform (SDNCP v4.2).

5.2 Security Standards

  - Encryption: AES-256 for data in transit; TLS 1.3 minimum for all management interfaces
  - Authentication: Multi-factor authentication (MFA) required for all administrative access; compatible with Microsoft Azure Active Directory and Okta SAML 2.0
  - Monitoring: Integration with Meridian's existing Splunk SIEM platform via syslog (RFC 5424)

5.3 Hardware

NexaTel Edge Gateway NE-5000 specifications:
  - Throughput: 10 Gbps full-duplex
  - WAN interfaces: 4x 10GbE SFP+
  - LAN interfaces: 8x 1GbE RJ45
  - CPU: Intel Xeon D-2183IT, 16-core
  - Memory: 64 GB DDR4 ECC
  - Storage: 480 GB NVMe SSD
  - Operating System: NexaTel NexOS 7.4.2

SECTION 6 — POLICIES

6.1 Acceptable Use Policy

Customer agrees to abide by NexaTel's Acceptable Use Policy (AUP), available at nexatel.com/legal/aup, as updated from time to time. Prohibited uses include, but are not limited to: spam transmission, port scanning, unauthorized network probing, and facilitation of illegal activity.

6.2 Network Management Policy

NexaTel reserves the right to implement reasonable network management practices consistent with the FCC's Open Internet Order (Report and Order on Remand, Declaratory Ruling, and Order, 30 FCC Rcd 5601 (2015)) and applicable law.

6.3 Data Retention Policy

NexaTel will retain call detail records (CDRs) and network logs for a minimum of 18 months in accordance with applicable legal requirements and NexaTel's internal data governance policies. Meridian may request CDR exports at no additional charge.

6.4 Change Management Policy

Any changes to network configuration, IP address assignments, or service parameters must follow NexaTel's Change Advisory Board (CAB) process. Emergency changes require approval from both Sarah Chen and David Okonkwo. Standard changes require 72-hour advance notice.
