ARTICLE 1 — PRODUCTS AND SERVICES

1.1 Contracted Products

Axiom Software Technologies sells the following software products to Consolidated Logistics Partners under this Agreement:

  a) Axiom FleetCommand — Enterprise fleet management platform. Licenses: 800 named users.
  b) Axiom RouteOptimizer Pro — AI-powered route planning and logistics optimization engine. Licenses: 500 concurrent users.
  c) Axiom DataVault — Secure cloud data warehouse solution. Storage: 50 TB allocated.
  d) Axiom SupportDesk — ITSM and ticketing platform. Licenses: 150 agents.
  e) Axiom Analytics Studio — Business intelligence and reporting suite. Licenses: 250 named users.

1.2 Professional Services

Axiom provides implementation and training services:
  - Platform onboarding (FleetCommand + RouteOptimizer Pro): 120 hours
  - Data migration from legacy SAP TM system: 80 hours
  - Custom API integration with CLP's Oracle ERP: 60 hours
  - End-user training sessions (Houston, TX headquarters): 5 days

ARTICLE 4 — TERM AND RENEWAL

4.1 Initial Term

This Agreement has an initial term of three (3) years, effective February 1, 2024 through January 31, 2027.

4.2 Renewal

This Agreement automatically renews for successive one-year terms unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal at least ninety (90) days prior to the then-current expiration date.

4.3 Renewal Pricing

Subscription fees for each renewal term are subject to an annual increase not to exceed the greater of: (a) 5% or (b) the CPI increase for the prior year as published by the U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics.

ARTICLE 6 — TECHNOLOGY AND INTEGRATION

6.1 Technology Stack

Axiom's platform uses the following core technologies:
  - Backend: Python 3.12, FastAPI, PostgreSQL 15, Redis 7.2
  - Frontend: React 18, TypeScript
  - Infrastructure: Kubernetes on AWS EKS, Terraform
  - Monitoring: Datadog APM and Infrastructure
  - Data pipeline: Apache Kafka 3.6, Apache Spark 3.5

6.2 Integration Requirements

CLP's integration landscape that Axiom products depend on:
  - Oracle E-Business Suite R12.2 (ERP) — Axiom FleetCommand depends on Oracle ERP for financial data
  - SAP Transportation Management 9.6 (legacy, being migrated) — RouteOptimizer Pro depends on SAP TM for historical route data
  - Microsoft Azure Active Directory — all Axiom products use Azure AD for SSO/SAML 2.0 authentication
