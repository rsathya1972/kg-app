INTERNAL ANNOUNCEMENT — ORGANIZATIONAL UPDATE
Engineering & Platform Division

From: Elena Vasquez, Chief Technology Officer
To: All Engineering & Platform Staff
Date: March 10, 2024
Subject: Q1 2024 Org Restructure — New Reporting Lines, Team Assignments, and System Ownership

---

OVERVIEW

Following the acquisition of Brightbridge Analytics and the launch of our new AI Platform initiative, we are realigning the Engineering & Platform Division effective April 1, 2024. This announcement covers new reporting lines, team memberships, system ownership transfers, and office location changes.

---

SECTION 1 — LEADERSHIP CHANGES

1.1 New Reporting Structure

The following changes take effect April 1, 2024:

  - James Okafor (previously VP Engineering, Platform) is promoted to SVP Engineering. James will now report directly to Elena Vasquez (CTO). James owns the overall engineering roadmap and is responsible for the Platform, Data, and AI teams.

  - Priya Subramaniam joins as VP of AI Platform (external hire from DeepMind). Priya reports to James Okafor. Priya leads the newly formed AI Platform team and owns the company's internal machine learning infrastructure, including ModelServe (our internal ML model serving platform) and the FeatureStore pipeline.

  - Daniel Kowalski (previously Director of Data Engineering) is promoted to VP of Data Engineering. Daniel reports to James Okafor. Daniel's team belongs to the Platform Division and owns DataFlow (internal ETL orchestration system built on Apache Airflow 2.8) and the Analytics Data Warehouse (running on Snowflake).

  - Ravi Choudhury (previously Senior Manager, Backend) becomes Director of Backend Engineering. Ravi reports to James Okafor. Ravi's team belongs to the Core Engineering Division.

  - Sophia Brennan joins as Director of Site Reliability Engineering (external hire from Stripe). Sophia reports to James Okafor. Sophia owns Kubernetes infrastructure (our k8s cluster running on GKE), the Prometheus + Grafana monitoring stack, and PagerDuty alerting configuration.

  - Marcus Lindqvist remains as Director of Security Engineering, now reporting to Elena Vasquez directly (previously reported to James Okafor). Marcus owns the Vault (HashiCorp Vault Enterprise) secrets management system and the SIEM platform (Splunk Enterprise Security).

1.2 Brightbridge Analytics Integration

Following the Brightbridge Analytics acquisition (closed February 14, 2024):

  - Tara Goldstein (formerly CEO of Brightbridge Analytics) joins as VP of Product Analytics. Tara reports to Elena Vasquez. Tara's team belongs to the Product Division.

  - Kevin Marsh (formerly CTO of Brightbridge Analytics) joins as Principal Engineer, Analytics Platform. Kevin reports to Daniel Kowalski. Kevin brings the Brightbridge Insight Engine, which belongs to the Analytics product suite and will be integrated with the Analytics Data Warehouse.

SECTION 3 — SYSTEM OWNERSHIP TRANSFERS

Effective April 1, 2024, the following system ownership transfers are confirmed:

  - ModelServe: owned by Priya Subramaniam's team (AI Platform). Previously owned by James Okafor.
  - FeatureStore: owned by Ben Harrington (AI Platform). Depends on DataFlow.
  - DataFlow (Apache Airflow 2.8): owned by Daniel Kowalski (Data Engineering). Other teams depend on DataFlow for data feeds.
  - Analytics Data Warehouse (Snowflake): owned by Daniel Kowalski.
  - Brightbridge Insight Engine: belongs to Analytics product suite; owned by Kevin Marsh; integration with Analytics Data Warehouse scheduled for Q2 2024.
  - Orders API: owned by Fatima Al-Hassan; depends on PostgreSQL 15 and Redis 7.
  - Kubernetes infrastructure (GKE): owned by Sophia Brennan.
  - Vault (HashiCorp): owned by Marcus Lindqvist; all services governed by company secrets management policy SM-POL-003.
  - Splunk SIEM: owned by Marcus Lindqvist; governed by Security Incident Response Policy SIR-POL-001.
